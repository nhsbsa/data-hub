Provider Assurance is a programme within the NHS Business Services Authority that helps ensure healthcare providers—such as dentists, pharmacists, and optometrists—are delivering services in line with NHS rules and standards.

It supports NHS England by providing oversight, identifying risks or irregularities, and helping ensure public funds are used appropriately. Through data analysis, reviews, and collaboration with providers, the team works to maintain the quality, fairness, and integrity of NHS services
