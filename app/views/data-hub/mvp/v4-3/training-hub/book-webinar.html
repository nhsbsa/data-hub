Online webinars

We offer a free webinar training service to all users. You can book onto these sessions as many times as you need.

{% call details({ summaryText: "Electronic prescribing analysis and cost tool (ePACT2) online webinars" }) %}

General Introduction

A general tour of the ePACT2 system, highlighting the data being used within the system and a demo of some of the pre-built content available in ePACT2. This is an introductory session aimed at new users of ePACT2 operating at any organisational level (National/Regional/ICB/SICBL/Trust/PCN/GP Practice).

Introduction to Building Analysis

This webinar gives instruction on how to build your own analysis in ePACT2 using the data available to registered users of the system. This will include a demonstration of building an analysis and manipulating the results of the analysis into visualisations. This session is aimed at new users to ePACT2 who want to create their own analysis within the system.

Bespoke help/training session

Do you need specific help with something ePACT2 related? Have you attended the General Introduction to ePACT2 webinar and Building an Analysis in ePACT2 webinar (if needed)? If so we can offer a 30 minute 1-2-1 session with one of our experienced trainers. We do ask that you give us as much information as to what you want to cover in advance of the session so that we can ensure you get the most benefit from attending.
