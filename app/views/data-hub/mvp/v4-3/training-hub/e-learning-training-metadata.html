Electronic prescribing analysis and cost tool (ePACT2) Introduction

Last updated: 18 September 2025

Author: Daniel Grieve

What does this training cover?

This training provides a general overview of ePACT2 highlighting the data being used within the system and a demonstration of some of the pre-built content available within the system.

What will I learn from this training?

After completing this training you should:

  • be confident with understanding what ePACT2 is and how to log on
  • understand what data is available with the system
  • understand the support resources available to you
  • be confident navigating the system to view dashboards and reports
