The Training Hub is a centralised location, providing access to a range of educational resources and e-learning. All our training resources have been developed by professionals to support you in the best way we can.

Who can use the Training Hub?

The Training Hub is available for all registered users of NHSBSA Data Services products and services to support you in accessing the content available on the Data Hub.

Users can access the Training Catalogue for learning resources such as e-learning and video tutorials to assist you in accessing the data you require.

You can book a place onto one of our online training webinars, where a member of the training team will take you through a specific data area your interested in, such as the Prescribing and Dispensing Data area, with a demo of content available to users.
