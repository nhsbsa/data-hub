Our method

Data on the number of NHS sight tests performed, and the number of vouchers, supplements, repairs, or replacements, along with data around eligibility indicators submitted on the GOS forms.

Data is presented at multiple organisational levels, including National, Regional, ICB, Contractor and Performer.

Data product details

Product owner and data steward

  • Michael Duncan (Product Owner)
  • Rob Haynes (Senior Information Analyst)

Time period

January 2014 to March 2025

Geographic coverage

Limited to organisations held as GP practices in our organisational database.

Organisational level

Data available at National, Regional, ICB, Contractor and Performer levels.

How often is the data updated

Updated monthly, in line with the eOPS refresh.

Data lineage/provenance

Data is provided to the NHSBSA by the data provider Primary Care Support England (PCSE) on behalf of the data controller NHS England.

No patient information is included in the dashboard. Risk of reidentification of patients is low.

Purpose is a activity management and cost analysis for NHS organisations. To be used by appropriate NHS professionals.

Caveats and limitations

Data is limited to GP practices and where we have calculated that there is a potential saving based on the costs of the products prescribed. Comparison of cost appears to be the current price vs the price in the month specified. The data may not be representative of recent changes in price. No suppression has been applied, access to the dashboard is limited to registered users.

Citing and reusing this data

If you plan on producing a press or broadcast story based upon the data, contact our communications team. This is important to ensure that the figures are not misunderstood or misrepresented.
