Electronic Ophthalmic (eOPS)

Through this service you can view data collected by Primary Care Support England (PCSE) and files sourced from the Open Exeter and EGOS systems.

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

Registered users across England can view and analyse ophthalmic data.

Reports are automatically updated with the latest available data. All data can be exported to Excel, PDF and PowerPoint.

Understanding eOPS data

What data is available?

Activity

  • treatment data carried out by ophthalmic providers (including amount of activity broken down by GOS form type)

Eligibility

  • patient-level eligibility indicators are available within the dashboards (including glaucoma risk, receiving income support, universal credit, and registered blind)
