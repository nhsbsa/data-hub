Methodology statement

Data on the number of NHS sight tests performed, and the number of vouchers, supplements, repairs, or replacements, along with data around eligibility indicators submitted on the GOS forms.

Data is presented at multiple organisational levels, including National, Regional, ICB, Contractor and Performer.

Time period
April 2018 to May 2025
Geographic coverage
Limited to ophthalmic data submitted by contractor with England.
Organisational Level
Data available at National, Regional, ICB, Contractor and Performer levels.
Update Frequency
Updated monthly, in line with the eOPS refresh.
Data lineage / provenance
Data is provided to the NHSBSA by the data provider Primary Care Support England (PCSE) on behalf of the data controller NHS England. No patient information is included in the dashboard. Risk of reidentification of patients is low. Purpose is a activity management and cost analysis for NHS organisations. To be used by appropriate NHS professionals.
