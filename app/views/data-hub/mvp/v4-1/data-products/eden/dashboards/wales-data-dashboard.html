Methodology statement

Data on the activity of Welsh dental contractors at Local Health Board (LHB) level for the current year to data. Data is based on information submitted by dental contractors via the ComPASS system.

Data product details

Product owner and data steward
  • Michael Duncan (Product Owner)
  • Liam Kitchingside (Senior Information Analyst)
Time period
Year to date data for the latest financial year.
Geographic coverage
Limited to dental data submitted by contractors within Wales.
Organisational Level
Data available at Local Health Board level.
Update Frequency
Updated monthly, in line with the eDEN refresh (usually around the 21st of the month).
Data lineage / provenance

Data is sourced from eDEN, FP17 data submitted to the NHSBSA by contractors via the ComPASS system.

No patient information is included in the dashboard. Risk of reidentification of patients is low. Purpose is activity management and cost analysis for NHS organisations. To be used by appropriate NHS professionals.

Caveats and limitations

Information:

Data included within the dashboard is limited to activity data submitted by the contractor via the ComPASS system and available within eDEN.
