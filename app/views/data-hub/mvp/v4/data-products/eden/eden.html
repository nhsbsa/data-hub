Dental Data (eDEN)

The service will allow you to access interactive and easy to use dental data reports and dasboards

eDEN is an online application designed to help users access dental data more efficiently. It enables quick retrieval of information, supports user-friendly report management, and allows you to refresh and export both dashboards and flat data tables with ease. With eDEN, you can also view interactive data visualisations, making it simpler to understand and explore key dental insights.
