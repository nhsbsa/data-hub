Electronic Prescribing Analysis and Cost Tool (ePACT2)

The service will allow you to access interactive and easy to use prescription data reports and dasboards. Data is available 6 weeks after the dispensing month

Designed to support better decision-making, it allows authorised users to explore and analyse prescribing trends, costs, and patterns through interactive dashboards and custom reports.

Whether you're in a clinical, commissioning, or financial role, ePACT2 helps you make data-driven decisions to improve patient care and deliver value to the NHS.

Understanding ePACT2 data

Data is available 6 weeks after the dispensing month.

An explanation of the fields held in the system is available on the Data Dictionary.

You can use the age logic summary flowchart for information on how patient age is determined in ePACT2.

ePACT2 contains data down to single prescription level. If you have access to this level of data, remember that you cannot share confidential data outside of your organisation. For this reason, you should read and agree to the ePACT2 User Agreement as part of the registration process.
