We provide information on dental data to registered users through dashboards and reports.

These products can help to identify trends and inform decisions to provide value for money services.

This service allows you to:

  • quickly access dental data
  • review reports
  • refresh and export dashboards and flat data tables
  • view data visualisations
Understanding eDEN data

Those eligible can access FP17, Compass and dental performer level data. Dashboards and reports are automatically updated with the latest available data.

All data is exportable to Excel, PDF and PowerPoint.

Who can access this data?

NHS colleagues in England and Wales.

Register for eDEN

What data is available?

Contractual

  • information on dental contracts (including which companies or individuals hold these contracts)
  • details of practitioners working on each contract

Activity

  • treatment data from dental surgeries (including type of treatment and performer)

Financial

  • Total contract value, remuneration, pensions and business rate information

Access

  • Patient access levels across England and Wales
  • Historic levels

Risk

  • Risk models have been developed to identify contract outliers and risks to both the provision and the quality of dental care
